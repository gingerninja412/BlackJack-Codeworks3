How to play

  1. Each player is dealt two cards
  2. You can choose to take another card or to stop where they are
  3. Your goal is to get a higher combined value than the computer or to hit 21
  4. If you go over 21 you have gone bust and lose the game
  5. Aces are worth 1 or 11 depending on what is needed
  6. All picture cards are worth 10
